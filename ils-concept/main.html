Capture phase. The aircraft establishes the localizer and glide path, transitioning from general alignment to a stabilized instrument guidance state before the final approach segment.

Approach refinement. Lateral and vertical guidance dominate the flight path, while the runway environment, threshold cues, and centerline alignment become increasingly distinct.

Completion / Stabilized Landing

ILS LANDING

The landing sequence is stabilized and held for the final instructional emphasis. This scene remains fixed at the end of the presentation without looping.
